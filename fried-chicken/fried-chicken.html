Fried Chicken

Description

Who doesn't love some good fried chicken? Well here's a recipe page minus the actual recipe, cause it is far too long to type.

Steps

  1. Wash your chicken.
  2. Season with salt, pepper, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder etc.
  3. Let it marinate for 1 hour.
  4. Make a seasoned flour dredge and coat chicken well.
  5. Fry the chicken.
  6. Enjoy!
