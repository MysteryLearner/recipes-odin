Pizza

Description

Pizza, oh pizza. Depending on the recipe, you are one of the most comforting foods to me. Anyways, did you actually think I was gonna write steps on how to make pizza?

Steps

  1. Make dough.
  2. Leave it to rise for around an hour so.
  3. Spread the dough and add cheese and tomato sauce.
  4. Put in oven and take out when golden brown and cheese melts.
  5. Voila! Enjoy.
